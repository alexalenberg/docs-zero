## Q&A

**Can Flatpeak access any sensitive data on my account?**\
No. We can only see your address and energy account information (like tariff details, contract type, and billing overview). Some providers may display the last 4 digits of a saved card, but full details are not visible. We only look at tariff data ‚Äî nothing else.

**Do you make any changes to my account?**\
Never. We don‚Äôt make changes, and we don‚Äôt store your personal information. We only check how your provider displays tariff data, and test that this data could be securely shared if you connect a device (like an EV or heat pump).

**Can I change my password afterwards?**\
Yes, absolutely. Many people create a temporary password before sharing. We ask you to keep it active for up to 2 months, as sometimes we need to re-run tests, but we‚Äôll let you know once everything is complete.

**When will I receive the reward payment?**\
As soon as we confirm your login details work, we‚Äôll send your reward the same day. Amazon vouchers usually arrive instantly, while bank transfers can take a couple of working days to show in your account.
