The Flatpeak supports deletion for several object types.

When an object is deleted, related objects may also be removedâ€”or their references unlinked.
This page outlines how deletion behaves across different object types and relationships.
## Customer

When a [customer is deleted](/api-reference/zero/customers/delete-a-customer):

- All Locations belonging to this Customer are also deleted.

## Location

When a [location is deleted](/api-reference/zero/locations/delete-a-location):

- The Customer it belongs to is updated to remove the deleted Location.
- All Tariffs associated with this Location are deleted.

## Tariff

When a [tariff (element) is deleted](/api-reference/zero/tariffs/delete-a-tariff-element):

- The Location object it was associated with is updated.
- All related Connect information is deleted, including energy provider connection data (keys, credentials, etc).

## Device

When a [device is deleted](/api-reference/zero/devices/delete-a-device):

- No other objects are updated.
