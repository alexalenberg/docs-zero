---
title: Exporting your data from Flatpeak
sidebarTitle: "Exporting your data"
---

You have full ownership of your data stored in Flatpeak as outlined in the [DPA](/legal/data-processing-agreement). When you need to export your data, you can use [Customers](/api-reference/zero/customers), [Locations](/api-reference/zero/locations) and [Tariff elements](/api-reference/zero/tariffs) endpoints to download all customer data stored in our systems.

Please contact [support](https://flatpeak.com/support) for further help with exporting your data.
