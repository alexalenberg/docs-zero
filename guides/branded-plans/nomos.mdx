This guide explains how to enable your customers to connect their [Nomos](https://nomos.energy) plan via your integration with Flatpeak. 

<Steps>
<Step title="Setup your account with Nomos">

Before you begin, you need to:

- [Get in touch with Nomos](https://www.nomos.energy/en/demo) to create an account with them.
- Once you received your `client_id` and `client_secret` request adding https://nomos.connect.flatpeak.energy to authorized redirect URI
- Follow [Authorization Code Grant](https://docs.nomos.energy/authentication/authorization-code) process to make sure your account with them is functioning correctly.

</Step>
<Step title="Configuration in Flatpeak">

- Go to [Dashboard](https://dashboard.flatpeak.com/integrations/branded-plans/nomos), navigate to Integrations > Branded Plans > Nomos.

- Complete all necessary fields:
  1. Provide your Nomos `client_id` and `client_secret` 
  2. Input your brand name
  3. Upload your logo
  4. Tick the enable the integration checkbox.
  5. Click **Save**


- Run Connect, and when entering an address in Germany or Austria, your logo should appear on the provider selection page.

</Frame>

</Step>
</Steps>
