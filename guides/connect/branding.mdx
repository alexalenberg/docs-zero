When a customer connects their energy provider account, you must inform them how their data will be processed and stored. This is a legal requirement under GDPR and other data protection laws. For details, see the [Data Processing Agreement](/legal/data-processing-agreement).

To comply, go to the Flatpeak [Dashboard](https://dashboard.flatpeak.com/branding) and add the required links. **This step is mandatory**.
