title: Overview of energy scheduling APIs
sidebarTitle: Overview
---

Flatpeak’s energy scheduling APIs use our tariff dataset to generate optimal import/export schedules based on your power requirements—optimised for grid tariffs, carbon intensity, and local generation.

You can select from three API endpoints:

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Schedule by duration" horizontal icon="clock" href="/guides/scheduling/duration">
    Find lowest or highest price periods by specifying required duration of time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Schedule by price limit" horizontal icon="money-bill-trend-up" href="/guides/scheduling/limit">
    Find lowest or highest price periods by specifying acceptable price range.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Schedule by amount of energy" horizontal icon="battery-half" href="/guides/scheduling/amount">
    Create import/export schedule based on energy amount, duration and/or transfer rate.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
